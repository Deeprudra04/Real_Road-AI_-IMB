About Rice Leaf Disease Detection

AI-powered system for detecting rice leaf diseases

AI Technology

This application uses a deep learning model based on DenseNet121 architecture, trained on thousands of rice leaf images to accurately detect various diseases.

Disease Detection

The system can identify 6 different conditions: healthy leaves and 5 common rice leaf diseases with high accuracy.

Detectable Diseases

Healthy

Normal, disease-free rice leaves

Bacterial Leaf Blight

Caused by Xanthomonas oryzae pv. oryzae

Brown Spot

Caused by Cochliobolus miyabeanus

Leaf Blast

Caused by Magnaporthe oryzae

Leaf Scald

Caused by Monographella albescens

Narrow Brown Spot

Caused by Cercospora janseana
